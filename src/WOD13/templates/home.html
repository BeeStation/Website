WOD13, For Beginners

It appears you have stumbled into the cozy corner of the WOD13 website. It has the rules for the server, a server statistics page, a page to review server bans, a forum, and links to many more places you should visit!

WOD13 is a Space Station 13 server, hosted by Crossedfall, which was created in March 2018. Our codebase is built off of /tg/'s foundations, combining our own unique features with those found on other servers.

Our server is open source, so feel free to contribute to the code! We also have a Discord server with a great helpful community; a forum for ban appeals, staff applications, suggestions and discussions; as well as an informative Wiki.
